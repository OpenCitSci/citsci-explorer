Inventory of environmental citizen science projects

Publisher: European Commission, Joint Research Centre (JRC)

Data resulting from a desk research study ordered by the European Commission (DG ENV, with the support of DG JRC) in order to get an evidence base of citizen science activities that can support environmental policies in the European Union (EU). The study, entitled 'Study on an inventory of citizen science activities for environmental policies', was carried out by Bio Innovation Service (FR), in association with Fundacion Ibercivis (ES) and The Natural History Museum (UK).
